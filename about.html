Computer Engineering

Quick Facts
  • 📅 Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)
  • 🎓 Credits: 128 credits
  • 🔬 Laboratories: 8 specialized
  • 👥 Class Size: 25-30 students

Program Overview

Computer Engineering focuses on the design and development of computing systems, bridging the gap between hardware and software. Students learn to create innovative solutions from microprocessors to supercomputers.

First Year Courses
  • Introduction to Programming
  • Digital Logic Design
  • Calculus I & II
  • Physics for Engineers
  • Computer Organization
  • Discrete Mathematics
  • Engineering Drawing
  • Technical Communication
Program Features
Hardware-Software Co-design Embedded Systems Computer Architecture Network Systems Artificial Intelligence
Career Opportunities

Software Engineer, Hardware Designer, Systems Architect, Embedded Systems Developer, Network Engineer
