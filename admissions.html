Admissions Process

Your journey to becoming an Engineering College student starts here

Application Steps

1

Submit Application

Complete the online registration form with your personal and academic details.

2

Document Review

Our admissions team reviews your transcripts, test scores, and supporting documents.

3

Interview

Selected candidates participate in an interview with faculty members.

4

Acceptance

Receive your admission decision and enrollment package within 2-3 weeks.

Financial Aid & Scholarships

We offer various financial aid options to make engineering education accessible:

  • Merit Scholarships: Up to $5,000 per year for outstanding academic achievement
  • Need-Based Grants: Available for students demonstrating financial need
  • Work-Study Programs: On-campus employment opportunities
  • Engineering Fellowships: Department-specific awards for research and innovation
